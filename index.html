About Us

Welcome to Pet Finder! We're passionate about one thing here and thats finding animals their forever homes. Every town has animals in shelters and rescues that need adopting. I never looked for a dog on my own, but once I came across one that needed rescuing I took her in and never looked back. That's the same love and feeling we want to help pass on to anyone else looking for a new best friend! We want to help close that gap and get those numbers down! Whether its big, small, fluffy, or furry. We want to help you get that next member of the family home for the next Christmas card!

How it Works

Conveniently search for the nearest pet adoption center utilizing Google's state of the art search engine! If the town name you search doesn't match your town, try narrowing it down by searching via zip code!
